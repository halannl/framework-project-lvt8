**Theme Title:** Neon Highway Orbit

**Scenario:**
A dark highway curves forward beneath neon fog that pulses from magenta to deep blue, casting liquid light across wet pavement as distant cityscape towers dissolve into haze. The motion is circular and hypnotic—orbiting through lanes that feel like they're climbing upward while staying level, each curve pulling you deeper into the glow. Everything moves with controlled momentum, a steady acceleration that never peaks, just keeps rolling forward into endless electric dusk.
