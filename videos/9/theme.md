**Theme Title:** Silver Cloud Sea

**Scenario:**
An endless expanse of clouds stretches below like a silent ocean, reflecting silver-white light from a sun that hangs low and diffused through pale mist. The air is weightless and soft—everything floats in a slow, meditative hover above the cloud surface, where subtle shifts in brightness create waves of luminescence that ripple outward without urgency. Motion feels like drifting on a slow current, suspended in a space where sky and cloud merge into a single, breathable glow.
