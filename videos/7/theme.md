**Theme Title:** Deep Archipelago Glow

**Scenario:**
A low sun casts bronze-to-violet gradients across scattered island silhouettes that float in a sea of mist, each landmass barely visible through the haze. The air feels thick and intimate—light moves slowly, pooling in warm amber pockets before dissolving into cooler indigo depths. Everything pulses in a slow, hypnotic rhythm, hovering low and close to the surface, drawn downward into the glow rather than rising above it.
