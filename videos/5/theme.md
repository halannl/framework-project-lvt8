**Theme Title:** Rooftop Ember Sway

**Scenario:**
A warm ember glow washes across a rooftop skyline as the sun sinks past distant towers, casting long golden light that softens into rose and peach. The air feels open and easy—city lights flicker on below like scattered fireflies, while the sky holds its warmth a little longer. Everything sways gently in place, suspended in that golden-hour pause where day hasn't quite let go.
