**Theme Title:** Savanna Silk Flow

**Scenario:**
A low sun casts honeyed light across endless grassland that ripples like silk in slow motion, each wave breathing with the wind as if the earth itself is exhaling. The horizon stretches infinite and flat—golden warmth pools in the valleys between swells, while the sky above shifts from saffron to lavender with an unhurried grace. Everything flows outward in wide, organic arcs, expanding into open space with the steady rhythm of earth and air in dialogue.
