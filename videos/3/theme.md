**Theme Title:** Amber Canyon Descent

**Scenario:**
A fading amber glow sinks into the deep shadows of a winding canyon, where layered rock walls hold the last warmth of daylight in copper and rust tones. The air is still and close, carrying a hypnotic quiet as everything slowly dissolves from bronze to indigo. Motion feels like a gentle downward spiral, descending through strata of color with an intimate, grounded pull.
